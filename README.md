# WAREHOUSE ANALYSIS DASHBOARD

This Power BI project provides an operations analysis dashboard designed to monitor, track, and optimize warehouse performance. The goal is to equip supply chain, logistics, and warehouse management teams with a powerful tool to understand inventory movement, operational efficiency, and order fulfilment performance.

üéØ Business Objective
1. Warehouses are critical for ensuring smooth product flow, timely fulfilment, and controlled inventory levels. Inefficiencies directly lead to delays, stockouts, excess storage, and increased operational costs.
2. This dashboard addresses the need to proactively analyze warehouse operations and helps stakeholders answer key questions such as:
3. What are the total inbound and outbound quantities handled?
4. Which products or categories exhibit the highest movement?
5. How effective is the order fulfilment process?
6. Are there any stock shortages, overstock issues, or operational bottlenecks?
7. How does warehouse activity trend over time?

üíæ Data Source
The analysis is based on the Warehouse Analysis.csv dataset. It includes product details, inbound and outbound stock, warehouse locations, stock availability, and delivery status required for operational performance assessment.

üìä Dashboard Features & Key Metrics
1. The report visualizes warehouse KPIs and performance trends, providing a clear and instant understanding of operational efficiency.
2. Total Inbound vs Outbound Quantity: A high-level comparison of stock entering and leaving the warehouse.
3. Current Stock Levels: Displays available inventory across warehouses and product categories.
4. Category-wise Inventory Movement: A breakdown showing which product categories have the highest movement.
5. Warehouse Region Performance: Analysis of stock handling and operational efficiency across warehouse locations.
6. Order Delivery Status: Overview of orders delivered on time vs delayed, highlighting fulfilment performance.
7. Movement Trends Over Time: A time-series analysis to identify peak activity periods or workflow bottlenecks.
